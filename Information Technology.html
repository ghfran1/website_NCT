Information Technology Department

The Information Technology (IT) Department at New Cairo Technological University provides students with a strong foundation in computer science, programming, networking, cybersecurity, and data management. The department focuses on practical and applied learning, combining theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience in modern IT labs. Graduates are equipped with the skills needed to excel in the digital and technology-driven world, and they are prepared for careers in software development, IT management, network administration, cybersecurity, and other technology-related fields. The department also emphasizes research, innovation, and continuous professional development, ensuring students are ready to meet the demands of the local and global job markets.
