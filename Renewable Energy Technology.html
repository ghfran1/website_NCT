Renewable Energy Technology Program

Vision

An advanced educational and learning system that relies on modern technology to achieve continuous learning in the field of new and renewable energy, while maintaining international quality standards.

Mission

To prepare qualified technologists with integrated skills and knowledge according to the latest international standards, aligned with the requirements of the Egyptian labor market and scientific research in the field of new and renewable energy.

Objectives

  • Apply mathematical, scientific, and practical skills in solving technical problems.
  • Work efficiently in various renewable energy fields.
  • Demonstrate ethical and effective behavior in their professional environment.
  • Continuously develop their skills and knowledge.
