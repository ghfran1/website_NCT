Tuition Fees for Academic Year 2025–2026

As announced in August 2025, there will be no increase in tuition fees for the upcoming academic year.

The annual tuition fees for the Faculty of Industrial and Energy Technology are as follows:

Year / Category Annual Tuition (EGP)
Year 1 & Year 2 15,000 EGP
Year 3 & Year 4 20,000 EGP

Scholarship Programs

  • Merit Scholarships: For students with outstanding academic performance.
  • Scholarships for Students with Disabilities: Dedicated support for students with special needs.
  • Criteria: Some scholarships require maintaining a high GPA and may cover specific academic terms.

Important Notes

  • Students are encouraged to follow the official university website and social media pages for the latest announcements.
  • For detailed information, students may directly contact the Scholarship Office or the Admissions & Registration Office.
  • Tuition payments are made in Egyptian Pounds through the university’s official bank branches.
  • Scholarship discounts are applied by the Admissions & Registration Office after eligibility verification.
